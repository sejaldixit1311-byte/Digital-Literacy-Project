##### Task 5: Prevention-checklist 

##### Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist



\- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  

\- Always verify the sender before sharing personal information  

\- Use strong and unique passwords  

\- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)  

\- Never share OTPs or bank details  

\- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions  

\- Keep your apps and devices updated  



###### &#x20;UPI / Financial Safety:

\- Double-check UPI IDs before making payments  

\- Do not scan unknown QR codes  

\- Never approve unknown payment requests  



###### &#x20;Reporting:

\- Report cybercrime at https://www.cybercrime.gov.in  

\- Call Helpline: 1930 (24x7 support)
