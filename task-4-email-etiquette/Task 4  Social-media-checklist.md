#### Task 4 : Social-media-checklist



##### &#x20; Social Media Do's and Don'ts

###### 

###### Do's:



* &#x20;Maintain a professional profile  
* &#x20;Share useful and meaningful content  
* &#x20;Respect others online  
* &#x20;Protect your personal information  
* &#x20;Think before posting  
* &#x20;Use privacy settings properly  
* &#x20;Verify information before sharing  

###### 

###### Don'ts:

* Do not share sensitive information  
* &#x20;Avoid posting inappropriate content  
* &#x20;Do not engage in online arguments  
* &#x20;Avoid spreading misinformation  
* &#x20;Do not misuse social media platforms  
* &#x20;Avoid oversharing your location  
* &#x20;Do not click unknown or suspicious links
